How to Play

  • 🎯 Aim and Shoot: Click or tap to aim and shoot bubbles
  • 🎨 Match Colors: Connect 3 or more bubbles of the same color
  • 💥 Clear Groups: Matching bubbles will pop and disappear
  • 🏆 Strategic Play: Create chain reactions for bonus points
  • ⚠️ Don't Let Them Fall: Game ends if bubbles reach the bottom

Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse/Touch: Aim and shoot
  • ⌨️ D Key: Toggle debug information
  • ⌨️ I Key: Toggle performance info
  • ⌨️ R Key: Restart game
  • ⌨️ Space: Pause/Resume
